Q
WHO WE ARE?

Immolists is a Digital Media Company that owns and operates Real estate property portal website and complimentary offline media throughout Algeria. Immolists websites aim to make online real estate research easier and faster by simplifying access to it’s property listing plateform, whilst being one of the best tools to help purchase, sell or rent property.

Q
WHAT WE DO?

We provide real estate particular and professionals searchers, with useful resources throughout a large property listings platform that aims to facilitate contacts and communications towards any real estate transactions.

Q
How do your services work?

It’s simple, by subscribing into Immolists, you will not only have the access to review all the listed property in a real time but also have the access to post and list your property, thus share comment and request additional infos from others subscribers concerning their listings.

Q
What type of visibility with potential buyers and sellers does Immolists offer?

By creating and subscribing to our web site, you become one among thousands of subscribers in the Immolists community, and that offers you the possibility to communicate directly with other buyers and sellers, further more we have the property alerte.

Q
Does immolists enter as a third party intermediary in between buyers and sellers at the time of completing real estate transactions ?

No, Immolists as already mentioned on “Who we are” Section, doesn’t enter as a third party in any transaction, however it provides tools and listings that makes contact and communication between different actors (buyers and sellers, lessors and lessee) easier and simple.

Q
Do I Need to pay to post my property via Immolists?

Listing property services are completely free, however some additional optional services can be subject to some fees.
